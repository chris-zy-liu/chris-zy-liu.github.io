>On 10th Street music<br>turns carnival into a musical<br>befit for distance. by noon<br>those who were left behind had little<br>left to say so. we tread<br>deeply in concrete and sinks<br>just a bit - wishes were granted<br>for some water to sweat.<br>You said today is no different<br>than those other times where<br>music turned 10th Street<br>into an acquired taste. here again<br>is a pole you ought to reach for to die<br>quickly & mercifully. they will pray<br>in your name morbidly & dutifully. they’ll<br>say heaven(!) is overpopulated and<br>God surely cannot permit another<br>and another and another and another.<br>wishes were granted for some water<br>to be poured and clean the filth off of<br>your leather boots and 10th street<br>over and over and<br>over and over and.
